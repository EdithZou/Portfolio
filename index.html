About

Hello! I’m Tong Zou, a Ph.D. student at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My research focuses on 3D ultrasound reconstruction and digital markers for surgical navigation, with broader interests in biomedical imaging and AI-assisted healthcare technologies. Beyond research, I enjoy contributing to student communities through publicity, event organization, and peer mentoring. I also pursue photography and visual storytelling, capturing both landscapes and portraits to explore the intersection of science, art, and human experience. Welcome to my portfolio — here you’ll find highlights of my academic projects, student engagement work, and creative photography collection.
